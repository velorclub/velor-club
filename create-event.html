Tips for a good turnout

  • 01 Be specific about difficulty and what to bring — it sets expectations and builds trust.
  • 02 Pick a clear, well-known meeting point. Vague locations cause no-shows.
  • 03 A real cover photo (not a stock image) gets noticeably more RSVPs.
  • 04 Posting 5–10 days ahead gives people time to plan, especially for weekend trips.
